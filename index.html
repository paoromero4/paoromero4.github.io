About Me

Hi! I am a Mechanical Engineering student at MIT. I learn by making, and I make to keep learning. I am drawn to manufacturing, product design, and robotics, but I'll take on anything that lets me explore and experiment. Along the way, I have found I also love helping others learn through their own projects— whether it's through mentorship, collaboration, or hands-on guidance.

Outside of making, I play the oboe for the MIT Wind Ensemble and spend as much time as I can outdoors. I particularly enjoy rock climbing, hiking, camping, running, and open water swimming.
